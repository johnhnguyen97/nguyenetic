# Hero Image Attribution

The four hero background stills (`zen-01.jpg` through `zen-04.jpg`) are derivative works from photographs of the Ryōan-ji zen rock garden in Kyoto, Japan, obtained from Wikimedia Commons.

**Original photographs:** Courtesy of multiple contributors on Wikimedia Commons, sourced from Category:Ryōan-ji.

**Modifications applied:** Each image was resized to a maximum dimension of 1920px on the long edge and recompressed as JPEG at quality 85. No cropping, color grading, or other modifications were applied at the asset level. Color tinting and darkening visible on the live site are applied at render time via CSS overlays in `src/components/sections/Hero.tsx` — the source files remain unmodified beyond the resize/compress operation.

**Source category:** https://commons.wikimedia.org/wiki/Category:Ry%C5%8Dan-ji

**Temporary status:** These images are a stopgap. The intended final assets are AI-generated "digital zen garden" renders produced via the Nano Banana (Gemini) MCP, which will replace these files when Gemini API credits are available.
